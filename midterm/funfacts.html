fun facts!

  1. There are 88 officially recognized constellations
  2. The word “constellation” comes from a Latin term meaning “set with stars"
  3. While stars may appear super close they are action hundreds of light years away from each other, and us. That means we are seeing light from hundreds of years ago!
  4. The largest constellation is Hydra, taking up most of the night sky.
  5. Stars appear to move in the sky when really they are fixed in space. The reason they appear to move is because of Earth's rotations
